About fragrance:

Fragrances are created by mixing perfume oil with alcohol; the more perfume oil, the stronger the fragrance and the longer it will last. EDP is the strongest type of perfume we sell. Eau de Parfum contains between 10-20% of perfume oil, and is a popular choice with both perfume brands and customers.

Bleu De Channel

Bleu de Chanel by Chanel is a Woody Aromatic fragrance for men. Bleu de Chanel was launched in 2010. The nose behind this fragrance is Jacques Polge.

Creed

Absolu Aventus by Creed is a fragrance for men. This is a new fragrance. Absolu Aventus was launched in 2023. Top notes are Grapefruit, Bergamot and Black Currant; middle notes are Ginger, Cinnamon, Citron and Cardamom; base notes are Pink Pepper, Patchouli and Vetiver.
